Our Story

Since 2019 our private dog shelter has been a home for more than seven hundred abandoned dogs rescued from the streets or abusive environments. We are a passionate and dedicated team of volunteers, constantly striving to provide the best care and love these pure souls deserve.


Our Mission

Our mission is to find a forever loving home, for every dog sheltered with us. We promote responsible adoption and are committed to match our four-legged friends in need with loving families, who can welcome them home and offer them affection and care for a lifetime. Together we can change their lives! Be part of this journey! Offer one of our lovely dogs a home and you will receive endless unconditional love and gratitude!
